Branding - Perfume

Branding - Perfume

Poster

This individual project is a perfume branding.

The slogan seen on the poster "Let the light into your life" refers to the enlightened emotions the brand aims to evoke in customers.

The idea behind placing the logo not on top of a sticker like most the perfumes, but engraving it on the transparent surface allows the light shine through the glass, creating magical colors and heart-warming, romantic, delightful emotions. This picture also appear on the product poster, where the perfume is presented with a rising sun in the background, which gives the glass beautyful light colours.

Brand Name

The Brand Name Lumiere Essence comes from French, which means: the essence of light.
The Typography: Gustatory Delight Regular mirrors the elegantly feminine side of the product.

Logo

The Logo also represents this idea with a minimalistic, elegant symbol of the sun.
The primary logo includes the brand name as well, the secondary logo, only with the sun symbol is applied on small surfaces.

Behind the Scenes

This picture below is just a small insight behind the scenes. The method of creating the background was inspired by a new method in the film industry, where huge LED screens are used behind the actors instead of green screens, which makes the scene more realistic and easier to create. Being excited from this solution, I applied the process in small, using my laptop as a LED screen behind the small mockup bottle.
