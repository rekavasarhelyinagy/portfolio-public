UX/UI Design

The purpose of this group project was to practice developing a digital product of a public transportation app with focus on the user experience. During the project, we conducted research, performed usability tests, analyzed data, and did prototyping to create a product that is more aligned with the needs and more feasible for the end user.

Competitor Analysis

As a first step, we analyzed our competitors, as it is crucial to understand their strengths and weaknesses to find a way to stand out from them.
Our direct competitors included the local public transportation app, which we analised in more details, conducting user tests on it to understand what steps need to be improved.

User Research & Analysis

We conducted interviews with users to understand their experience with the existing app. Based on the data collected, we created personas to represent different types of users.
We also documented their steps and creating a user journey to document and better understand the process.

Choosing the Colour Scheme

We decided to choose dark blue as the background as it is more pleasing to the eye with white text having a high color contrast.
We have found during our research, that one of the most important things for the users is having an app they can trust, therefore we have chosen the colour blue, which is the symbol of trust.
Orange is the main color of our logo. We decided to choose this over yellow, because it can add a touch of excitement and enthusiasm to our app and it also has connections to transportation, since it symbolizes energy and movement.
