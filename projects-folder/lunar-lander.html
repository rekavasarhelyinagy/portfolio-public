Lunar Lander Game

Lunar Lander Game

In this school project, the task was to code a game, where the mechanics was given. The player was supposed to slow the avatar down, so you don't hit the surface at too high velocity.
I have chosen a sea theme and have put some obsticles like jellifish, coralls and moving sharks to make the game more challenging. Avoiding these obstacles, you need to reach the landing surface and land with less than the given velocity.
