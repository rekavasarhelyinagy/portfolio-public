Branding - Bookstore

Branding - Bookstore

The background of this group project was, that in theory, a bookstore wanted to go digital, while keeping its value and identity of the traditional store. We first agreed on the core values and vibe on the bookstore, which we kept in mind in the whole design process. Then we created a brandname, logo, typography, brand colours, illustrations, moodboard, mockups, and an Instagramm prototype, where we posted our events, and marketing strategies as well.

Instagramm Prototype

While designing the Instagramm profile, we aimed to reflect our cozy vibe, using the brand colours, illustrations and typography.
The posts also show the bookstore’s marketing strategies and the community building social events too.
